Idea is to create a ChatGPT wrapper that comes up with ideas for Taekwondo exercise drills.

Use a form that has the following inputs
belt level
exercise_area (upper or lower body)
equipment (kicking shield (big target), hand targets, partners)
weekly_curriculum (form, breaking, kicking combinations, self defense)

Send the following input to ChatGPT
Give me a taekwondo exercise drill for {belt_level} students that focuses on {exercise_area}, uses {equipment} and will help for {weekly_curriculum}.
